Magnetic field B = 1.25 T

Rotation rate  = 20 rpm  (revolutions per minute)

Number of turns of coil N = 100

Total resistance of coil Rcoil = 10 W

Resistance of external load connected between A and B Rload = 125 W

 (A)

w = 20 rpm = (20)(2p) / (60)  rad.s-1 = 2.09  rad.s-1

area of coil rotating in magnetic field  A = (12´10-3) (6´10-3 m) = 72x10-6  m2

Rtotal = Rcoil + Rload  = (10 + 125)  W = 135  W
