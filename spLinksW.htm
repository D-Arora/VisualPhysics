BOOKS

view

Douglas C Gaincoli: PHYSICS for Scientists and Engineers

One of the best 1st year university textbooks on coverage of topics and readability. An excellent book for any teachers or student.

 

Paul G Hewitt: Conceptual Physics

A fantastic high school physics textbook from the U.S.A. An excellent book for all teachers or student.

 

Richard P Feynman: Easy & Not-So-Easy Pieces

A book all physics teachers should read. You should get a copy for your school library.

 

Stephen Hawking & Leonard Mlodinow: The Grand Design

A book all physics followers should read. You should get a copy for your school library.

 

Chad Orzel: Breafast with Einstein

Great explanations of quantum physics a must read book. You should get a copy for your school library.
